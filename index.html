Chest

8 Exercises with varying sets, reps and rest times

Exercises listed with A/B are supersets and should be completed together

1A - Incline BB Bench Press : 3 Sets, 8-10 Reps, superset with
1B - Incline DB Fly : 3 Sets, 8-10 Reps, 60 second rest

2 - Decline BB Press : 3 Sets, 8-10 Reps, 60 second rest

3A - Incline DB Press : 3 Sets, 8-10 Reps, superset with
3B - Cable Crossover : 3 Sets, 8-10 Reps, 60 second rest

4 - Assisted Dips : 4 Sets, Reps until failure, 60 second rest

Crunches, Haning Leg Raises, Rope Crunches : 3 Sets, 20 reps, 30 second rest, rotating between each ab set
  • Cardio: After completion of exercises 10-20 minutes of LISS cardio on treadmill/bike/eliptical/rower
